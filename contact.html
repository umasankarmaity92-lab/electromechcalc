Get In Touch

Whether you've spotted an error in a calculation, want to request a new calculator, have feedback on an existing tool, or are interested in a business or advertising inquiry, we'd like to hear from you. ElectroMechCalc is maintained by a small team that actively reviews every message.

Email

The fastest way to reach us is by email: support@electromechcalc.com

We aim to respond to genuine queries — calculator bugs, formula corrections, and feature requests — within a few business days. Please include the specific calculator name and your inputs if you're reporting a calculation issue, as this helps us reproduce and fix it quickly.

What to Include in Your Message

  • Bug reports: the calculator page, the exact inputs you used, and what result you expected versus what you got.
  • Calculator requests: the type of calculation you need and, if possible, the formula or standard it should follow.
  • Business or partnership inquiries: a brief description of your organization and what you're proposing.

Response Time

We're a small, independent site, so please allow a few business days for a reply, especially for detailed technical questions. We read every message even if a personalized reply isn't always possible for general feedback.
